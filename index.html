Tokenomics of DigiTON

DigiTON has two primary tokens that drive its ecosystem: BITS (Reward Token) and DigiTON (Governance Token). Each token serves a unique purpose, ensuring sustainability and excitement within the game.

BITS Token (Reward pool)

The BITS token is the in-game currency used for:

  • Evolving your digimons.
  • Feeding and keeping them alive.
  • Participating in dungeons, farms, and other challenges.

It also serves as a reward token for battles and missions. All BITS are used internally, ensuring controlled burning and sustainable economics.

DigiTON Token (Governance)

The DigiTON token plays a critical role in the ecosystem. It is used for:

  • Purchasing boosters and special items.
  • Feeding the reward pool and maintaining liquidity.

This token ensures the economic balance of the game, providing sustainability and governance power to players.
